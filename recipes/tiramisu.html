Easy Tiramisu Recipe

Description

This is a quick and easy tiramisu recipe that a good friend's mother was kind enough to share with me. Don't tell her I'm sharing it here!

Ingredients

Filling

  • 6 egg yolks
  • 1 1/4 cups white sugar
  • 1 1/4 cups mascarpone cheese
  • 1 3/4 cups heavy whipping cream
  • 2 (3 ounce) packages of ladyfingers (approximately 24 ladyfingers total)
  • 1/3 cup Kahlua (or coffee / substitution of your choice)

Toppings

  • 1 teaspoon unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1 (1 ounce) square semisweet chocolate (shaved)

Steps

Before you begin, chill the mixing bowl you'll be whipping the cream in.

  1. Bring water to a boil in the bottom pot of a double boiler. Keep the top pot off of the boiler for now.
  2. Combine egg yolks and sugar in the top pot of the double boiler.
  3. Once combined, add top pot with egg and sugar mixture to the bottom pot and reduce heat to low.
  4. Stirring constantly, cook the mixture for about 10 minutes.
  5. After 10 minutes, remove the mixture from the heat and whip in the yolks until the mixture is thick.
  6. Add the mascarpone cheese to the whipped yolks and beat until combined.
  7. Pull your chilled bowl out of the refrigerator and whip the cream until stiff peaks are formed.
  8. Gently fold the whipped cream into the mixture and set aside.
  9. Line bottom and sides of your serving dish with lady fingers.
  10. Brush the lady fingers in Kahlua or your substition.
  11. Spoon half of the cream filling over the lady fingers.
  12. Now repeat adding lady fingers, brushing with Kahlua, and covering with filling until you run out of lady fingers or reach the top of your dish.
  13. Cover the top layer of filling with cocoa and chocolate shavings.
  14. Now cover your tiramisu and refrigerate for several hours. Preferably, refrigerate overnight.

That's it! Now you're ready to serve your homemade tiramisu.
